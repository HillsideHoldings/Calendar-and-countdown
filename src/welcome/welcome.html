You have successfully installed Calendar & Countdown for Chrome

Calendar and Countdown is very easy to use. You just left-click a date to countdown to it and display a badge in the extension row.

To add notes or colors to dates, and to count down to additional dates, right click to show the menu.

To see all available information about a specific date, hover over it with your cursor, and an information box will pop up.

To count the number of days between any to dates, simply click on the first date and drag your mouse to the second without releasing the mouse button.

That's all there is to it!
